Artificial Intellegence

Artificial Intellegence is a study that is concerned with analyzing and describing human processes and tasks. As well as designing tools that can understand, support or execute these processes and tasks. It is something you see everywhere, think about searching with Google, recommendations on Netflix, self-driving cars, language recognition with Siri, spam filters and robots. So, it is a study where you need to be able to work with a computer and know how to program. Therefore, doing this course will come in hand of I do end up doing this study. This study is my plan C, meaning, if I don't get accepted to Forensic Science, I will still take a gap year if that is possible, but if that isn't possible, I will go and study Artificial Intellegence.
