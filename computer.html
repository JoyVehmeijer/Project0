Artificial Intellegence is a study that is concerned with analyzing and describing human processes and tasks. It is something you see everywhere, think about searching with Google, recommendations on Netflix, self-driving cars, language recognition with Siri, spam filters and robots. So, It is a study where you need to be able to work with a computer and know how to program. Therefore, doing this course will come in hand of I do end up doing this study.
