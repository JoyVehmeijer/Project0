The reason why

Initially, I thought I would study forensic science and if I didn't get accepted I would take a gap year. During this gap year I will live in Salamanca for half a year, so that I would be able to speak Spanish fluently afterwards. The other half year would give me the chance to sign in for Forensic Science for a second time and hopefully get accepted this time and look more into other studies I might be interested in. There were a few other studies that I might enjoy as well, namely Industrial Design (industrieel ontwerpen) and Computer Science and Engineering. But I didn't sign up for these studies this year because the both of them have selections as well and in the Netherlands you are allowed to sign up for only two sttudies with a selection. Therefore, I would have to choose between Industrial Design and Computer Science and Engineering, while I didn't know enougn about those 2 and thus wouldn't be able to know which of the two I would enjoy the most.
