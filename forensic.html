Forensic Science is a study where you learn how to collect effidence on a crime scene. When a crime has been committed, forensic scientist come the crime scene and collect fingerprints, make pictures of the scene and the evidence and collect traces of DNA. Later, they process everything in the lab to figure out who commited the crime. This has to be done very precize, so that the evidence doesn't tampered with and isn't valid anymore. This also clarifies the outfit a forensic scientist wairs at the crime scene. A white suit that covers their whole body, a haircap, gloves for the hands and gloves for the shoes and a face mask. All these things make sure no DNA, fingerprints or body fluids gets on the crime scene and evidence that doesn't belong there.
